1. Our Print On Demand Service

We won't print offensive content, fake brands, or anything illegal. If your design violates these rules, we'll cancel your order without printing.

4. Production Timeline

Orders start printing 1-2 days after order confirmation. Custom designs may take a bit longer. You'll get email updates on your order progress.

5. Product Quality & Color

We use the best printing methods available. Small color differences may happen due to screen settings and fabric type. This is normal. Once printed, designs can't be changed.

6. Payment Options

We accept Cash On Delivery (COD) - pay when you receive your order. No advance payment needed.

Your order ships 3-5 business days after printing. We pack with care and send to your address. For orders, you pay the full amount + delivery charges at your door.
